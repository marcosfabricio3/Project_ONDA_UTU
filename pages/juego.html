ONDA TRIVIA — choose a difficulty, press “Start”, and answer 10 questions.

How to Play

  1. Choose a difficulty level (panel on the right).
  2. There are 10 questions per game, each with 4 options.
  3. Time per question: Easy 25s / Medium 18s / Hard 12s.
  4. Lifelines (Easy only): 50/50 and Skip.
  5. If time runs out, the answer is counted as incorrect and the next question starts.
